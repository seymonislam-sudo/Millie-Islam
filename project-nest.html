NEST

Producer & Co-writer · London · 2026 · Selected for The New Black Film Collective (TNC) XPO 2026

A short film exploring contemporary themes through an intimate character study, developed and produced as a festival-ready project with a small collaborative team.

Role & responsibilities

As Producer and Co-writer, Millie worked across development, production and delivery, shaping the story while coordinating the practicalities of the shoot.

  • Co-developed the script and story beats with the director.
  • Managed schedules, locations, permissions and on-set logistics.
  • Coordinated a small crew, call sheets and shot lists.
  • Oversaw delivery of final assets for festival submission.

Context & outcomes

NEST was conceived as a compact, festival-ready piece that could be produced within tight student timelines and resources, while still feeling visually and emotionally ambitious.

  • Selected for screening at The New Black Film Collective (TNC) XPO 2026.
  • Developed a clear production workflow from script to final cut.
  • Strengthened collaboration between writer, director and production team.

Process

  • Researched references and tone, building a shared visual language with the director.
  • Ran table reads and script revisions to clarify character arcs.
  • Planned a compact shooting schedule that balanced ambition with budget and availability.
  • Worked closely with the editor to shape pacing ahead of festival deadlines.
