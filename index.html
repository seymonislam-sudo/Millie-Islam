Profile & skills

Millie is a London-based media student at University College London, working across editorial, film and digital content. She has supported projects at the British Film Institute, Knight Hall Literary Agency and KidsArtUK, alongside leading production for UCL MODO Fashion Society.

Her experience spans research, copywriting, script development, event production and social media. She is particularly interested in audience-focused storytelling that connects cultural organisations with younger viewers.
