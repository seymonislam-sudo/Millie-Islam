Currently pursuing a BA in Media at University College London and averaging a First, I work across editorial content, film production, and creative direction. From producing a short film selected for screening at TNC XPO 2026 to leading large-scale fashion events, my practice bridges research-driven storytelling with hands-on production craft.

I'm drawn to work that sits at the intersection of culture and media — whether that's writing a feature article for Sight & Sound, directing editorial fashion shoots, or managing social platforms for arts organisations. I bring rigour, creative ambition, and a collaborative spirit to every project.
